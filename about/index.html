About

What we do

With our community care programme, our clients have the opportunity to participate in more stimulative timetabled activities which are carefully planned to keep them mentally and physically engaged, giving respite to their primary carers for some hours in the day.

We recruit staff such as Health Care Assistants as well as Support Workers. Our staff have significant expertise in supporting people with challenging needs and are dedicated to giving our clients and their families a much-needed peace of mind with an assurance that Tristar care has the ability to provide their loved ones with safe and compassionate care of very high standards. We have the confidence to challenge those who are not abiding by the code of conduct. We find ways to support staff who may be struggling to relate with clients. We identify areas in which clients may present challenging behaviours and compassionately, we work with to bring about solutions that will ensure our clients receive the levels care that they deserve.

Our staff receive extra training to equip them as they take the time to really get to know each individual, understanding their needs, interests and goals. Our agency provide the care and support which sees that our clients improve their wellbeing and independence to achieve their potential.
