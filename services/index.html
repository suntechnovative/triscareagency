Services

What we offer

We aim to conduct, behave and carry an attitude that the service users who use our services should expect. We aim to work responsibly in the knowledge that we have a duty of care. We ensure that the conduct of our team does not fall below the standards of Code. Protecting and making sure that no harm meets our client and the public is our priority as their safety and wellbeing are our responsibility.

Because we understand that communication is important, we promote open and effective communication with our clients. We find that being able to connect with our service users naturally and effectively, creates a better atmosphere and experience for all, which is why we try to match our clients with staff that they can connect with easily because our purpose is to help our clients improve their interactions with the world, their expression of self and ultimately enjoy a good quality of life.

We aim to uphold and promote equality, diversity and inclusion, treating each client as an individual who has the right to express themselves freely without judgment or prejudice.

Tristar care Promotes and upholds our clients privacy, dignity, rights, health and wellbeing. Our staff are trained to work in collaboration with their colleagues to ensure that a high quality, safe and compassionate healthcare and support service is delivered at all times. Our staff are also overseen by our own internal quality teams, this is to ensure a robust and responsive approach is in place to support our team members in the best possible ways, that they may in turn offer the best level of service to the clients.

We are committed to providing short and long-term care and support to young adults and adults across greater London.
